OpenEvent

OpenEvent is the AI event discovery platform that powers the MusicLive sites and will power many others in the future.

But it's more than that: it's about sharing and inclusivity and love. If it gets you—yes you—out to a gig to rock your socks off, that's the win.

This is how you will find out when your favourite bands, or artists you never even heard of, are playing near you.

Every one of your favourite venues and bands can benefit from this, for free.

It's about getting you off your sofa, losing yourself in the mosh pit, spending a couple of bob at the bar and merch table, and having a lovely time.

We're working with venues, promoters, artists and—most importantly—punters to do this. We don't expect to make a penny from it, but if we put a smile on a single face, that's a success.
