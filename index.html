OpenEvent

OpenEvent is the open-source AI event discovery platform that powers your next favourite day out.

It's about getting you off your sofa, losing yourself in the mosh pit, spending a couple of bob at the bar and merch table, and having a lovely time supporting grass roots venues and local, lovely people.

OpenEvent is free as in speech, and free as in beer.

We're working with venues, promoters, artists and—most importantly—punters to do this. We don't expect to make a penny from it, but if we put a smile on a single face, that's a success.

OpenEvent is made with love by Simon, Jane & Annie.
